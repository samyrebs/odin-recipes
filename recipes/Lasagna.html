Lasagna

(The sauce should look more orange/pink in reality)

Description

This lasagna recipe can be time-consuming but the results are more than worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped frsh parsley, divided
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Combine ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard
  2. Add pickle brine and mix immediately to prevent the mayo from curdling
  3. Add salt, garlic powder, black pepper, and the optional MSG
  4. Mix a final time and you're done!
