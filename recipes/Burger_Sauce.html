Burger Sauce

(The sauce should look more orange/pink in reality)

Description

This burger sauce is the perfect tangy condiment to accompany any burgers or fried foods. While being great on a burger, it is also an amazing pairing to fried chicken, french fries, fried fish, or anything else that needs an acidic/tangy bite to cut through richness.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons ketchup
  • 4 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 1 tablespoon pickle brine (straight from a jar of pickles)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • (Optional) 1/4 teaspoon MSG
  • A very small pinch of salt
  • A slightly larger pinch of pepper
